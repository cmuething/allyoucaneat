All You Can Eat 2000 is a shoppable variety show currently produced and performed in Ridgewood, Queens. The show features live music, comedy, interviews, and much more. The costumes, props, and sets of All You Can Eat 2000 are all for sale, always. Buy and watch, watch and buy, just watch, only buy, whichever you choose to do, the show will go on.

AYCE 2000 is scheduled for May 2022 at 682 Woodward Ave.
