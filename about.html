All You Can Eat 2000 is a shoppable variety show currently produced and performed in Ridgewood, Queens. The show features live music, games, interviews, and much more. Everything in the show, from the clothing & accessories to the props & sets are available to purchase. Buy and watch, watch and buy, just watch, only buy, whichever you choose to do, the show will continue.

AYCE 2000 is scheduled for August 2022 at 682 Woodward Ave.
